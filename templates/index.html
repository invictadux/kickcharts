Kick statistics and analytics

The page displays real-time Kick statistics, as well as the main record values for key metrics. Weekly and monthly statistics are also available here; it helps one get a basic idea of Kick audience behavior.

What are Kick stats?

Kick stats show the most relevant data by key metrics. In particular, the viewers now parameter gives an idea of how many people are on the Kick site right now. Similarly, channels now is a counts channels that are currently broadcasting live, etc.

How to check Kick Stats?

The main current Kick metrics are available on this page. If you need Kick analytics for a specific channel, just enter its name in the search bar and get its detailed statistics.

How do i see my Kick stats?

To find out your statistics, just use Kick stats tracker. Simply search for your nickname and proceed to your channel page on Kick Charts to get the information you need.

How do you find streamers stats?

Kick Charts collects Kick streamers stats from channels with 1+ Average Viewers. More details on the nuances of collecting data from other platforms can be found in the About section.

What is the average viewer count on Kick?

Average viewer count is a number of concurrent viewers watching one's stream. To calculate this figure, one needs to know how many viewers are watching the broadcast at any time throughout its entire duration.

What is a unique viewer on Kick?

Unique viewers count is an average value of registered Kick users to those who follow stream anonymously.
